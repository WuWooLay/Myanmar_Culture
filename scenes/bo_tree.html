Bo Tree

Written by U Than Pe ( Tour Guide )
Bo Tree

Whatever they see is new for visitors . Dress , birds , animals , plants insects , trees ; in fact everything that is native to the land is foreign to them . So no wonder they are curious They want to know about everything they see , be it a marriage ceremony novitation or the ubiquitous Bo tree . It is our duty to enlighten them. In spite of the fact that Bo tree is almost always found in the vicinity of pagoda very few visitors have asked me about the significance of proximity between the two. When we are asked there is a wide choice . On one end there is a short answer . Lord Buddha attained enlightenment at the foot of a Bo tree . On the other end there is a long story . Starting with Buddha-to-be when he wished and vowed to be a Buddha one day , and ending with enlightenment at the foot of the Bo tree . I always take the midway . Here's the summarized form.

Gotama , the Buddha-to-be was born in 623 BC' . He was born in Kapilavastthu . His parents were of Sakyan Clan . They were King Suddhodana and Queen Maha Maya. He married to Princess Yasodhara of Devadaha . He and His bride wew both 16 . At the age of 29 they got a son Rahula . On the same day. He saw four signs aged , sick , death and hermit . He left the pleasures and comforts of a princely . He went to Uruvela forest searching for eternal truth . For six years He practised dukkhacariya . At the age of 35 He attained enlightenment at the foot of the Bo tree near the town of Gaya . That resulted in the close connection between Buddism and Bo tree . A Bo tree in Sri Lanka is supposed to be associated with the original Bo tree from Gaya . Presenting a Bo sapling sariradhtau relics from one country to another is a great tradition among Buddhist people . Receving people accept them with reverence . Bo sapling , are planted near the prominent pagodas.

Other related trees belonging to the Ficus spicies are found all over the country . Bo tree is of course Ficus religiosa . Ficus obtusifolia bears edible fruits . Ficus infectoria gives leaf-buds that are used as lettuce and vegetable . Ficus indica has drop roots handing from its boughs . Ficus nervosa and Ficus citrifolia are both calles nyaung peinee,bear beautiful fruits belived to have medicinal value.But they are incedible.Ficus elastica is called fig tree and bears edible fruits.They are good as cure for dysentry . Ficus palmata bears inedible figs . Ficus elastica give latex which is turned into rubber like elastic . Children play with it and use it to catch birds Ficus glomerata grows near wetlands and is called ye tha phan . Ficus heterophylla is the only member of this specices found as shrub. Ficus (NYAUNG) is a generic name of group of trees given above . They are found all over the country . Most of them are deciduous . Their new shoots appear only after all old leaves are shed . The name nyaung has become part of Myanmar culture , religion tradition and language.
