Planetary Post And Astrology

Written by U Than Pe ( Tour Guide )
Planetary Post And Astrology

Myanmars got Buddhism from India , that is Bharat . Along with religion the country also got Sanskrit and Pali words . In fact she got many other ideas and objects too . Astrology is one of those . First wave came much earlier . It brought in older form of Buddhism mixed up with Ari practice . According to some authorities it entered the country , by land . Second wave came with Shin Arahan and Anawrahta's conquest of Suvannabhumi . It introduced Teravada form of Buddhism into Bagan Empire .

Since the dawn of Pyu civilization the country adopted Sasana calendar based on Indian astronomy and astrology . Subsequent civilizations and rulers dropped some years from that era and started their own eras . Those cut-offs from Sasana calendar made our Myanmar Era shorter by 1182 years . When later Myanmar conquest of Kachar and Manipur brought in Manipuri Brahmin Pundits to the Myanmar King's court . Most of them were accomplished astrologers . They reinforced peoples' trust and dependence on astrology and astrologers .

Basic astrology was almost always taught as part of monastic education system . We started with thin bone jee the Myanmar orthography . We moved on to Mingala Sutta ( Mangla Suttra ) . After that most of us left monastery and joined the rural workforce . A few of us continued with namakara , paritta , loka niti before leaving . Some of us continued as novices and went on to join sangha order , and Don the saffaron robe for life . But all of us learned basic astrological maxims by heart by reciting them in unison every night before going to bed . Because of those maxims most of us can calculate the days , the directions , the appropriate and opportune moment for travel or starting of a venture . In short we all were well-versed in basic astrology .

Myanmar astrology is based on seven days of the week , But there are eight points of compass ; east , west , north , south , and four corners . The problem is solved by dividing the fourth day of the week into two parts . As a result Wednesday is bisected and one half is given to Rahu . Now it is possible to give eight planets each a position . At most great pagodas there are planetary posts .

So we start from Sunday corner , the north-east . The reigning animal is garuda . Its planetary age is 6 years . Its alphabet is lonely ah giving the sound of A . So sunday borns will have their names starting with A , like Aung Minn , Aye Mu etc.. . Its heavenly body is sun .

Monday has the east . The reigning animal is tiger . Its planetary age is 15 years . Its alphabets are ka, kha , ga and nga . So Monday borns have a wide choice of names , Kyaw Kyaw , Kalya , Khin Maung , Chaw Tsu , Jack or Jenny etc.. for a start . Its heavenly body is moon .

Tuesday is given the south east corner . The reigning animal is Lion . Its planetary age is 8 years . Its alphabets are tsa , hsa , za , and nya , a lot of variety to choose from . Tsan Maung , Tsein Tsein , Hswe Myint , Hsu Mon , Zaw Lwin , and Nyo Nyo , Literally thousands of choices . Its heavenly body is Mars , the angry red planet .

Wednesday is given the south . The Wednesday has been split into two parts . Only first is recognized as under the reign of animal elephant . Its planetary age is 17 . Its alphabets are la and wa , a narrow choice comparatively; Win Maung , Hla min etc.. . Its heavenly body is Mercury .

Thursday has to skip over the south west corner and settle at the west end . The reigning animal is mighty rat so far no method to exterminate it has succeeded . Its planetary age is 19 . Its alphabets are pa , pha , ba and ma , a fairly wide choice . Thursday borns may call themselves Paw Oo , Pa Pa , and Mu Mu . Its heavenly body is Jupiter .

Friday is given the northern front . The reigning animal is guinea pic . Its planetary age is 21 years . Its alphabets are tha and ha . Friday borns can call themselves Thaung Dan , Thi Thi , and Han win or Honey . Its heavenly body is Venus .

Saturday's corner is the south west . The reigning animal is the serpent . Its planetary age is 10 years . Its alphabets are ta , hta , da and na giving the Saturday borns a goodly choice ; Tin Oo, Htwe Htwe , Daniel , Debora , Norton and thousands of other names . Its heavenly body is Saturn .

Before we take up the odd creation of Rahu let us have look at Saturday corner of Shwedagon Pagoda . Normally people sit and meditate and count beads at their birthday corner . But when we come to Saturday corner we find more people counting beads . Then the uninitiated asks the question : "Are there more Saturday borns in Myanmar ? "

Here we have to explain that money , cash or wealth belongs to Saturday . As most people want wealth they sit at Saturday corner . It is not their birthday corner , but their corner of hope or deliverance from want . So , naturally we find more people at Saturday corner .

Now let us have a look at Rahu , the second half of Wednesday . Its corner is the northwest . Its reigning animal is tuskless variety of elephant . Its planetary age is 12 years . Its alphabets are lajee and ya . So people born on the second half of Wednesday are regarded as Rahu borns and hence they may be named Lay Maung , Yin Maung or Yin Shwe . It has no heavenly body to represent it . But astrologers take the mother Earth as Rahu .

Our Myanmar people are very conscious of their birthdays . For other people birthday comes once a year . But for us it comes every week . Most of us abstain from meat , eggs and fish on our birthday . ( Note : For easy aid to memory nine-square grid given above maybe used . Remember , East , West , North and South Mukhas have two planetary post each . )
